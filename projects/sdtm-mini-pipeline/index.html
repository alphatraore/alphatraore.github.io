SDTM Mini-Pipeline (Raw → SDTM domains)

Reproducible pipeline transforming raw EDC-style datasets into SDTM domains with controlled terminology, ISO8601 date handling, and quality checks.

What it does

  • Builds SDTM domains (DM, AE, EX, VS, LB)
  • Handles partial dates/times and ISO8601 formatting
  • Applies controlled terminology where applicable
  • Generates logs/check summaries for quick review

Tools: SAS / R, CDISC SDTM, Git
